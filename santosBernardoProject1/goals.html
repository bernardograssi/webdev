Wonderful Goals Scored by the Seleção in World Cups

#1 Carlos Alberto Torres vs. Italy in 1970

Known as the greatest team goal of all time, this goal outputs the great team quality from that Brazilian team in 1970. The ball passed through 9 players, ending up with a superb strike from legendary right-back Carlos Alberto Torres.



#2 Pelé vs. Sweden in 1958

One of the greatest goals scored by the King Pelé, this goal scored after a sombrero flick over the Swedish defender in 1958's World Cup Final when he was only 17 years old is an iconic moment in the history of the World Cups.



#3 Sócrates vs. USSR in 1982

After taking two defenders away, Sócrates sent a wild rocket towards the Soviet goalkeeper's top right corner. This goal from Dr. Sócrates' was one of the most beautiful ones scored in 1982.



#4 Ronaldinho vs. England in 2002

One of the most iconic free-kick goals of all time, this strike from Ronaldinho from 40 yards away caught English goalkeeper David Seaman by surprise and led Brazil to the 2002 World Cup Final. Ronaldinho still claims to this day that he never had the intention to cross the ball, but to score from there.



#5 Eder vs. Scotland in 1982

Legendary winger from Atlético-MG, Eder scored this piece of beauty against Scotland in 1982. Known as a player with a powerful shot, this time Eder did not put much power on the ball, just looped the goalkeeper, who was caught out of position.



#6 Garrincha vs. Bulgaria in 1966

The King of Dribble, as he was known worldwide, Mané Garrincha scored this superb free-kick goal against Bulgaria in the 1966 World Cup. Known for his quickness, agility, and cleverness when driblling, Garrincha also knew how to score goals, and this one is the definite proof.



#7 Careca vs. France in 1986

This brilliant finish from the Brazilian striker Careca was the best possible way to end the fantastic play created by the Brazilian players. Brilliant exchange of passes and quick movements led to a marvellous goal, which is still remembered to this day.



#8 Josimar vs. Northern Ireland in 1986

Long shots cannot get any better than this. Brazilian right-back Josimar struck the ball with perfection and found the top right corner of the Northern Irish goalkeeper, who stood no chance.



#9 Eder vs. USSR in 1982

Another astonishing strike from Eder appears in the list, this time against USSR in 1982. After a beautiful dummy play from legendary midfielder Falcão, Eder struck the ball mid-air with perfection to score against the Soviets, who had no luck against that powerful Brazilian team in 1982.



#10 Nelinho vs. Italy in 1978

Similarly to Eder, Nelinho was well known for his powerful shots, but also for his "outside of foot kicks". In this goal against Italy in 1978 we can see his amazing technique in hitting the ball, which curls away from goal, and then out of the sudden, curls towards the goal. This iconic "trivela" goal is the perfect demonstration of his outstanding technique.
